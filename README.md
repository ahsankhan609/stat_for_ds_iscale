## Course Description
This comprehensive course on statistics for data science is designed to take you from a beginner to an advanced level in understanding and applying statistical concepts in the field of data science. The course covers a wide range of topics, including descriptive statistics, inferential statistics, probability theory, hypothesis testing, regression analysis, and more. By the end of this course, you will have a solid foundation in statistics and be able to apply these concepts to real-world data science problems effectively. Whether you're new to data science or looking to enhance your statistical knowledge, this course is perfect for you!
